Overview


For the uninitiated, Ladakh is a region located in the state of Jammu and Kashmir, India. The city lies on the Leh-Srinagar highway and is little more than 400 km from Srinagar, the summer capital of Jammu and Kashmir. Irrespective of where you come from or whatever faith you practice, Ladakh provides something for everyone.Tourists love this place all year round. Unarguably, one of the most beautiful places on Earth, Ladakh, located in the northern reaches of India, is a place ripe with sights to delight and nature to astound you.
